How to Use:

  • Enter the amount of cash provided by the customer in the "Cash Received" field.
  • Click "Purchase Item" to calculate the change.
  • The system will display the change due or alert if there's insufficient funds.
  • Status indicators: OPEN = change can be given, CLOSED = exact change given, INSUFFICIENT_FUNDS = cannot provide change.
