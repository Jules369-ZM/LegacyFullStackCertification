A cellular automaton devised by the British mathematician John Horton Conway

Rules of Life

Birth

Dead cell with exactly 3 live neighbors becomes alive

Survival

Live cell with 2-3 live neighbors survives

Death

Live cell with <2 or >3 neighbors dies

How to Play

  • Click cells to toggle them alive/dead when stopped
  • Use presets for interesting starting patterns
  • Click "Random" for a random starting configuration
  • Watch as generations evolve according to the rules
