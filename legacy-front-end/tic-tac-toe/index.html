How to Play

  • Players take turns placing X's and O's on the 3x3 grid
  • Get three of your marks in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) to win
  • If all 9 squares are filled without a winner, it's a tie
  • Click "New Game" to start over or "Reset Score" to clear the scoreboard
